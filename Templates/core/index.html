Frequently Asked Questions

What is Billmink?

Billmink is a platform that allows users to easily receive payments from clients by simply sharing a payment link.

How long does it take to receive payments?

Billmink users are expected to get credited in their bank accounts after 1 working day.

What payments method does Billmink accepts?

Billmink currently accepts any currency as means of payments but users only collect payments in Naira .

How do I receive payments using Billmink?

To receive payments simply login to your dashboard and click on the New Bill button, generate a bill payment link, and share it with your client via email, SMS, or social media. Once your client clicks on the payment link, they will be redirected to a secure payment page, where they can enter their payment details and complete the transaction. You will receive the payment directly into your Billmink account, which you can then withdraw or transfer to any bank account.

Is Billmink secure for receiving payments online?

Billmink is popularly used by Nigerian freelancers, and small business owners to collect payments from clients and customers all over the world. It integrates with Paystack API to handle transactions to give users a secured payment experience .
